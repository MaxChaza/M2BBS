NBM = non-blocking multiplicity

A Petri net has the property of non-blocking multiplicity if the minimum weight of every incoming arc of a place is greater or equal to the maximum weight of every outgoing arc of that place.

Mathematically speaking: ∀ p ∊ P : •p ≠ ∅ ∧ min{ f(t,p) | ∀ t ∊ •p } ≥ max{ f(p,t) | ∀ t ∊ p• }.
