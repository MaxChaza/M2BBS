This property holds if the incidence matrix of the Petri net is equal to |SCCS| - 1.

The quotient set of the transitive closure of the structural conflict relation is called Set of Coupled Conflict Sets (SCCS). For more information on SCCS and SEQS see, e.g., [Silva, Recalde; Petri nets and integrality relaxations: A view of continuous Petri net models.; 2002].
