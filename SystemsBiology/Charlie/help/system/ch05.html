In this chapter you find the abbrevations for the different properties that are used by Charlie.

PUR = pure

A Petri net is pure if there are no two nodes, connected in both directions.

Mathematically speaking: ∀ x, y ∊ P ∪ T : f(x,y) ≠ 0 ⟹ f(y,x) = 0.
